About Us

Lumira is an application that simplifies the process of combining data from multiple sources, visualizing it, analyzing trends, and sharing info graphics and data stories, in the cloud, on the server, and on your desktop. A one stop self-service solution that allows analysts and decision makers to access, transform, and visualize data. With a simple interface that allow users to swiftly analyze data without the need for scripting.

Lumira benefits users by reducing the amount of time it takes to access and upload data from multiple resources. A quick and easy way to get up and running quickly as no software or hardware is needed. The extremely clean and highly intuitive interface makes creating visually pleasing and informative charts easy for users, no matter their experience level.
