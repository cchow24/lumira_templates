About Us

Lumira is an application that simplifies the process of combining data from multiple sources, visualizing it, analyzing trends, and sharing info graphics and data stories, in the cloud, on the server, and on your desktop. A one stop self-service solution that allows analysts and decision makers to access, transform, and visualize data.

Lumira benefits users by reducing the amount of time it takes to access and upload data from multiple resources. A quick and easy way to get up and running quickly as no software or hardware is needed. The extremely clean and highly intuitive interface makes creating visually pleasing and informative charts easy for users, no matter their experience level.

Our Mission and Vision

Lumira's vision is to help the world run better and improve people’s lives. Our mission is to help every customer become a best-run business. We do this by delivering technology innovations that address the challenges of today and tomorrow without disrupting our customers’ business operations.

Our Goal

Lumira has strong ambitions for sustainable business success, both for our company and for our customers. We believe the most important indicators to measure this success comprise both financial and non-financial indicators: revenue, margin, customer loyalty, and employee engagement.

Our Strategy

Organizations are now entering a new era of business model innovation, made possible by the convergence of cloud, mobile, social, and in-memory technologies. We believe that simplicity is the key to solving the challenge of business complexity, we can help unlock our customers’ innovation potential.
