March 13: Lumira 1-1 Workshop

March 13, 2015

Speaker: Adam Kelly

Meet SAP Experts in Workshops and Events. Whether we create the event to help you learn, or participate in a community driven event we are there to help you learn SAP Lumira.

In this workshop, we will help you take your first steps with SAP Lumira in the world of data visualizations. Our SAP Lumira expert will guide you one on one through the end-to-end process of unlocking the full potential of your local data. We will show you how to create stunning, interactive visualizations, how to build engaging stories and infographics, and how to share those throughout the organization via SAP Lumira Cloud.
