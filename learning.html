Learning: Beginner

Tutorial 1: Getting Started with Lumira

Take your first steps with Lumira in the world of data visualizations. We will guide you through the end-to-end process of unlocking the full potential of your local data. You will then be shown on how to create interactive visualization and build engaging stories and infographics, and how to share those throughout the organization via Lumira Cloud.

Tutorial 2: Visualizing Big Data

Here, we will proceed further into the details of connected data by merging your local data set with one from an BI Universe. This is followed by more advanced data preparation steps to manipulate and refine your data. We will then visualize the data in a story and infographics but this time with more advanced functions and techniques than shown in Tutorial 1

Tutorial 3: Sharing Across Your Organization

We will show you how to share your data story across your entire organization. In addition, we will introduce best practises and basis design principles to keep in mind when building your data story using infographics and story board. Sharing your story will be done using Cloud and we will guide you through the entire process.
